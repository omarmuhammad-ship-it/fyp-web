About DESCOM

A collaborative design platform built for creatives.

DESCOM.com is a collaborative design community where unfinished ideas are welcomed and developed together. Instead of only posting final work, users can share designs in progress, receive feedback, and explore new directions through comments and visual redesigns. By allowing the community to sketch, add elements, and build upon each other's ideas, DESCOM.com turns feedback into an interactive creative process, encouraging collaboration, fresh perspectives, and more thoughtful human-driven design.
